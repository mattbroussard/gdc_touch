BWI

Building Wide Intelligence is a research project developing a multi-robot system designed to help inhabitants of the Gates Dell Complex (GDC). A team of heterogeneous autonomous robots, both ground and aerial, serves as a platform for students and researchers to develop applications for assisting visitors, students, and staff in GDC. Walking visitors to someone's office, delivering packages, bringing papers from the printers, or coffee to the students are some of the applications we are developing. As new students use the platform in their classes new ideas have been proposed: social media interaction (the robots have Twitter accounts!), surveillance, event photography... and dancing!
